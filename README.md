📚 Shiksha Setu – Bridging the Gap in Learning
🚀 Overview

Shiksha Setu (which means “Bridge to Education”) is built with a simple idea:
👉 No student should be stuck just because they don’t have internet.

It’s an offline-first AI-powered web app that helps students continue learning even in low or no connectivity situations. Whether it’s revising concepts or clearing doubts, Shiksha Setu makes sure support is always within reach.

🎯 The Problem

In many parts of India, students face real challenges outside the classroom:

Internet access is unreliable or unavailable
Private tuition is expensive
Teachers aren’t always accessible after school
Parents may not be able to help academically

So when a student has a doubt…
👉 there’s simply no one to ask.

Over time, these small doubts turn into big learning gaps.

💡 Our Approach

Instead of depending fully on the internet, Shiksha Setu works around it.

When offline → students can still learn and save their doubts
When online → the app fetches AI-powered explanations automatically

It’s designed to work with real-world conditions, not ideal ones.
